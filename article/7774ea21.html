∂(ρuj)∂t+∂∂xi(ρuiuj)=−∂p∂xj+ρgj+μ∂∂xi∂uj∂xi+(μv+13μ)∂∂xj(∂um∂xm)\frac{\partial (\rho u_j)}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}(\rho u_i u_j) = - \frac{\partial p}{\partial x_j}+\rho g_j + \mu \frac{\partial }{\partial x_i} \frac{\partial u_j}{\partial x_i} + (\mu_v + \frac{1}{3}\mu)\frac{\partial}{\partial x_j}(\frac{\partial u_m}{\partial x_m}) ∂t∂(ρuj​)​+∂xi​∂​(ρui​uj​)=−∂xj​∂p​+ρgj​+μ∂xi​∂​∂xi​∂uj​​+(μv​+31​μ)∂xj​∂​(∂xm​∂um​​)

∂(ρuj)∂t+∂∂xi(ρuiuj)=−∂p∂xj+ρgj+μ∂∂xi∂uj∂xi\frac{\partial (\rho u_j)}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}(\rho u_i u_j) = - \frac{\partial p}{\partial x_j}+\rho g_j + \mu \frac{\partial }{\partial x_i} \frac{\partial u_j}{\partial x_i} ∂t∂(ρuj​)​+∂xi​∂​(ρui​uj​)=−∂xj​∂p​+ρgj​+μ∂xi​∂​∂xi​∂uj​​
